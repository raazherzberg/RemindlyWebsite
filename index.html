Remindly

Meet. Remind. Connect.

A MyMe based app that enriches your social encounters.

  • The Remindly mobile app

    helps you better configure you needs and reminders including manually inserting reminders for the next time you meet.

  • Smart data management

    helps you keep in touch with your friends.

  • Pebble smartwatch

    Using the Pebble smartwatch we are able to notify the user in a quiet and un-disturbing way.

  •  Facebook Integration

    Connecting to Facebook in a seamless way enables us to provide the user with relevant, up to date, and immediate information about his friends.

  •  Myme face recognition

    opens a wide range of new possibilities to enhance our real-world social experience

Remindly is an AWSOME app which approaches our everyday world in a CREATIVE way.
During the last 24 hours we managed to implement an example of what we believe could be a powerful tool in a quickly approaching augumented reality world.
